Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I make, brew, or use loose leaf tea?



  2. How much loose leaf tea to use per cup?

    Use 1 teaspoon per cup of tea. To be more exact, use one heaping teaspoon per 6-7 ounces of water. A lot of people use a tea mug and should use 2 tea spoons or two tea bags because a mug holds around 12 ounces of water. This is an average, some teas require more and some require less. For a teapot use one teaspoon for each cup of water and add one for the pot.


  3. How long should I steep loose leaf tea?

    White Tea: 1 to 2 minutes
    Green Tea: 2 minutes
    Oolong Tea: 3 minutes
    Black Tea: 3 to 4 minutes
    Darjeeling: 3 to 4 minutes
    Pu-erh: 3 to 4 minutes
    Rooibos Tea: 5 minutes
    Herbal Tea: 5 to 7 minutes
    Yerba Mate: 5 to 7 minutes
    Fruit Tea: 5 to 7 minutes

  4. How much tea will loose leaf tea make?

    2 ounces makes 20 to 25 cups
    4 ounces makes 20 to 25 cups
    8 ounces makes 40 to 50 cups
    1 pound makes 100 to 200 cups
